Use Case: Lifestyle and Home Corp

Providing sustainable and eco-friendly home decor, furniture, and other home products.
Planning to expand while enhancing customer engagement through a loyalty points program.

In this class, we are going to implement a loyalty points scheme that rewards customers for their purchases. Customers will earn points for every order they place, with the number of points calculated based on the total amount of each order. These points can later be redeemed for discounts or special offers, encouraging repeat purchases and fostering long-term customer engagement.

Product Search

Effective product discovery is essential for a smooth shopping experience. Lifestyle and Home Corp's storefront search is optimized to meet customer expectations:

  • Filter products by attributes like price, size, finish, and eco-friendly features.
  • Segmented into categories such as furniture, home decor, and outdoor.
  • Search results are tailored for different stores.

Cart Functionality

Lifestyle and Home Corp implements features to reduce cart abandonment and enhance conversions:

  • Anonymous carts created when a customer adds an item.
  • Customer should have the option to signin and anonymous cart must be merged with the existing customer cart, if there is one.
  • Custom line items show the loyalty points customer is set to earn.

Order Processing

Orders are processed efficiently with integrated APIs:

  • Integration with Connect applications that automatically updates the customer's profile with earned bonus points once an order is placed.

Data Customization Options

The customers should have the option to see their loyalty points as saved ina custom field in their personal profile.

Conclusion

By implementing a solution incorporating product search, cart management, and streamlined order processing, Lifestyle and Home Corp will enhance its customer experience and position itself for global expansion in the eco-friendly home products industry.
